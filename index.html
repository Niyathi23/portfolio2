Edujoy

Edujoy is a mobile application that introduces a gamified approach to enhance the learning experience of primary to high school students. It offers interactive video lessons, educational games, progress tracking, a leaderboard, assignment uploads, online tests, study materials, a discussion forum, and a to-do list. By combining game elements with comprehensive features, Edujoy aims to make learning enjoyable, engaging, and effective.

Pawsconnect

PawsConnect is an innovative project connecting individuals with injured or abandoned animals to nearby veterinarians, rescue centers, and volunteers. Through an intuitive platform, users can upload pictures and find immediate assistance, while also engaging with a blogging community focused on animal welfare. PawsConnect aims to create a compassionate network that offers both practical aid and a sense of community for those passionate about helping animals in need.
